## AI for Consultants: Scale Expertise Without Scaling Headcount

### Research & Intelligence
- Synthesize annual reports, earnings calls, and industry publications in hours
- Map competitor positioning from public sources
- Structure TAM/SAM/SOM analyses from secondary research

### Deliverable Production
- Meeting notes → AI outline → polished deck in 2 hours instead of 2 days
- AI drafts executive summaries, SWOT analyses, and strategic recommendations
- Generate Excel model structures and financial commentary

### Client Management
- Automated performance reports connected to live data sources
- AI summarizes action items and decisions before each client call
- First-draft proposals and statements of work from a project brief

## Protecting Client Confidentiality

**Never paste raw client data into consumer AI tools.**

Safe alternatives: enterprise AI tools with data agreements, local AI models, anonymized data, or Azure/Google Vertex AI with processing agreements.
