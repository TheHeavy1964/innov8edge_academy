## AI for HR & Talent Management: From Reactive to Strategic

### Recruiting & Talent Acquisition
- **Job Description Writing:** Generate inclusive, SEO-optimized job posts in 2 minutes
- **Resume Screening:** Score and summarize hundreds of applications against your exact requirements
- **Candidate Outreach:** Personalized outreach sequences that don't feel automated
- **Interview Prep:** AI-generated role-specific interview questions and scoring rubrics

### Onboarding & Learning
- **AI Onboarding Bot:** Answer every new hire question 24/7 in any language
- **Personalized Learning Paths:** Adaptive training that adjusts to each employee's role and pace
- **Policy Q&A:** Instant, accurate answers to HR policy questions without a ticket

### Retention & Performance
- **Churn Risk Modeling:** Identify at-risk employees 3-6 months before resignation
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Analyze engagement survey responses to detect team-level concerns
- **Performance Review Assistance:** AI-drafted review summaries from manager notes

## The AI HR Ethics Checklist

Before deploying AI in any HR process, verify:
- [ ] **Transparency:** Candidates and employees know AI is used in the process
- [ ] **Human oversight:** A human reviews all AI-assisted decisions
- [ ] **Auditability:** You can explain every decision if challenged legally
- [ ] **Bias testing:** Your AI tool has been tested for demographic bias
- [ ] **Data privacy:** Employee data is handled under appropriate legal frameworks (GDPR, CCPA)
