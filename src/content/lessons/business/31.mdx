## AI for Ecommerce: Automate, Personalize, and Scale

### Content at Scale
- **Product Descriptions:** Generate SEO-optimized copy for hundreds of SKUs in hours, not months
- **Ad Copy:** Create multiple variants of Facebook, Google, and email ad copy for split testing
- **Category Pages:** Write keyword-rich category and collection page content automatically

### Customer Experience
- **AI Support Bots:** Handle order status, returns, FAQs, and shipping queries 24/7
- **Personalized Recommendations:** Surface the right products to the right customer at the right moment
- **Review Response:** Auto-generate professional responses to product reviews at scale

### Operations & Revenue
- **Dynamic Pricing:** Adjust prices based on inventory, demand, and competitor data
- **Demand Forecasting:** Predict which products to stock more of before peak periods
- **Return Prediction:** Identify high-return-risk orders before they ship to add proactive communication

## Your Ecommerce AI Quick-Start Checklist

- [ ] Generate AI product descriptions for your top 50 SKUs
- [ ] Set up a chatbot on your site to handle the top 5 support questions
- [ ] Connect your store to an AI email tool for cart abandonment sequences
- [ ] Use AI to analyze your best-selling products and write more listings like them
- [ ] Test AI-generated ad copy against your current copy in a simple A/B test
