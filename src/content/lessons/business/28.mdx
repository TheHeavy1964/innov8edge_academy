## AI for Small Businesses: Your Competitive Equalizer

Large corporations have teams of marketers, analysts, and customer service reps. AI gives small business owners access to the same capability at a fraction of the cost.

## The Small Business AI Toolkit

### Tier 1: Get Started Today (Free or < $30/month)
- **ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini** — Content writing, customer emails, business analysis
- **Canva AI** — Marketing graphics and social posts
- **Google Bard / Gemini** — Research, competitive analysis, summarization
- **Calendly + AI** — Automated scheduling and intake

### Tier 2: Automate Your Workflow ($30-$100/month)
- **Zapier + AI** — Connect your apps and automate repetitive tasks without code
- **Make (formerly Integromat)** — Visual workflow automation for complex processes
- **Voiceflow** — Build a custom AI chatbot for your website
- **Mailchimp AI** — Personalized email marketing at scale

## The 30-Minute AI Audit for Your Business

Before you buy any tools, answer these three questions:

1. **What task do I do most often that requires no unique expertise?** *(email writing, social posts, invoicing)*
2. **What task creates the most bottleneck when I'm unavailable?** *(customer inquiry responses, appointment booking)*
3. **What data do I generate but never analyze?** *(sales patterns, customer feedback, website traffic)*

Each answer is an AI automation opportunity. Start with the task that costs you the most time or money — that's your first workflow.
