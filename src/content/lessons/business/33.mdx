## AI for Finance & Accounting: The Intelligent Back Office

### Bookkeeping & Transaction Processing
- **Auto-categorization:** AI learns your chart of accounts and categorizes transactions automatically
- **Bank reconciliation:** Match transactions to bank feeds and flag discrepancies in real-time
- **Receipt scanning:** OCR + AI turns paper receipts into categorized digital entries

### Accounts Payable & Receivable
- **Invoice extraction:** AI reads invoices (PDF, email, scan) and extracts fields with 99%+ accuracy
- **PO matching:** Automatically match invoices to purchase orders and flag discrepancies
- **Collections:** AI-generated payment reminders personalized to each client relationship

### Forecasting & Analysis
- **Rolling forecasts:** AI models that update automatically as new financial data comes in
- **Scenario modeling:** Generate best/base/worst case projections with one click
- **Cash flow prediction:** Predict 30/60/90-day cash positions from AR/AP pipeline data
- **Variance analysis:** AI-generated commentary explaining why actuals deviated from budget
