## AI for Restaurants: Serving Efficiency, Not Just Food

Restaurants operate on razor-thin margins (3-9%). AI isn't a luxury — for competitive operators, it's the edge that keeps the lights on.

## Where AI Delivers the Most Value

### Operations & Waste Reduction
- **Demand Forecasting:** Predict covers by day and time with 85%+ accuracy
- **Inventory Optimization:** Auto-generate supplier orders based on predicted demand
- **Staff Scheduling:** AI models that align staffing to predicted rush periods

### Marketing & Reputation
- **Review Management:** Draft personalized responses to every Google, Yelp, and TripAdvisor review
- **Social Media:** Generate weekly content calendars, post captions, and story ideas
- **Email Campaigns:** Personalized promotions to past diners based on their order history

### Menu Strategy
- **Menu Engineering:** Identify star items (high margin + high popularity) vs. dogs (low margin + low popularity)
- **Description Writing:** Craft sensory-rich menu copy that increases perceived value and average check
