# AI Memory & Context

Unlike humans, AI models don't have a "long-term memory" that they update in real-time. Instead, they have a **Context Window**.

## What is a Context Window?
The Context Window is the amount of information the AI can "keep in its head" at any given moment. This includes your current conversation and any documents you've uploaded.

### Token Limits
Memory is measured in **Tokens** (roughly 0.75 words). 
- GPT-4o has a window of ~128,000 tokens.
- Gemini 1.5 Pro has a window of up to 2,000,000 tokens.

### What Happens when the Window is Full?
The model uses a "First-In, First-Out" (FIFO) approach. It forgets the beginning of the conversation to make room for your latest message.

> **Crucial Concept:** This is why "summarization" is so important in AI automation—it helps keep the most important info inside the window.
