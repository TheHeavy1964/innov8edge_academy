# Identifying AI Opportunities

To be a successful AI builder, you must first be an **Opportunity Scout**. The secret to ROI is knowing when to use AI and when to stick to standard automation.

## Deterministic vs. Probabilistic

### 1. Deterministic (Standard Automation)
These are "If-This-Then-That" tasks. 
- **Rule-based:** 1 + 1 must always equal 2.
- **Example:** Syncing a contact from Typeform to HubSpot.
- **Tool:** Zapier, Python, standard scripts.

### 2. Probabilistic (AI Opportunity)
These are tasks where the "right" answer depends on context and patterns.
- **Reason-based:** "Write a catchy headline" has 1,000 right answers.
- **Example:** Analyzing a sales call to find "customer pain points."
- **Tool:** LLMs (Claude, GPT, Gemini).

## Finding the High-Impact Zone
Don't use AI for math. Use AI for **Meaning**. The highest impact AI opportunities are tasks that were previously impossible to automate because they required "human judgment"—like summarizing, translating, and ideating.
