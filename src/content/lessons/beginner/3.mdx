# Prompt Engineering Basics

Prompt engineering is the art of communicating with an AI model to produce a specific outcome.

## The Perfect Prompt Formula
A great prompt usually contains four elements:
1. **Role:** Tell the AI who it is (e.g., "You are an expert chef").
2. **Task:** What do you want it to do?
3. **Context:** Provide any background information.
4. **Format:** How should the answer look (e.g., a list, a table, a JSON object).

### Why Context Matters
The more context you give, the less the AI has to "guess." 

> **Pro Tip:** If the AI is giving you generic answers, it's usually because your prompt was too generic. Be specific!
