# Visual AI Workflows

Single-prompt AI is useful, but **Workflow-based AI** is transformative. By chaining together multiple steps, we can solve massive business problems.

## The Anatomy of a Workflow
1. **Trigger:** The "When" (e.g., New Invoice Received).
2. **Logic Nodes:** Filtering and sorting the data.
3. **AI Nodes:** The "Brain" (e.g., Extracting line items from a PDF).
4. **Action:** The "Result" (e.g., Sending data to QuickBooks).

### Linear vs. Branched Logic
- **Linear:** A straight line of tasks (A -> B -> C).
- **Branched:** If the invoice is > $10,000, send to the CEO for approval; otherwise, pay automatically.

## Building for Scale
Workflows allow you to automate the **mundane** so you can focus on the **strategic**. By visualizing your process on a canvas, you can spot bottlenecks and ensure your "Relay Race" is as efficient as possible.
