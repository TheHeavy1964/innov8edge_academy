# RAG & Vector Data

**Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)** is how we move from "Generic AI" to "Business-Specific AI."

## The RAG Workflow
1. **The Question:** The user asks something specific to your company.
2. **The Retrieval:** The system searches a **Vector Database** for the most relevant document chunks.
3. **The Augmentation:** The system stuffs those chunks into the prompt (as "Context").
4. **The Generation:** The AI answers based **only** on the retrieved data.

### Why Vectors?
Traditional databases look for exact matches (e.g., search for "Apple" finds "Apple"). 
Vector databases look for **meaning** (e.g., search for "Fruit" might find "Apple", "Banana", and "Orange").

## Grounding the AI
RAG is the #1 cure for AI hallucinations. By forcing the model to "Look it up" before it speaks, we ensure the answers are grounded in your actual business facts, not just probabilistic guesses.
