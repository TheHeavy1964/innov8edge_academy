## The Four Pillars of AI Observability

### 1. Performance Metrics (The "What")
Track quantitative system health:
- **Latency:** p50, p95, p99 response times
- **Throughput:** Requests per minute at peak load
- **Error Rate:** Percentage of failed requests
- **Token Economics:** Cost per conversation, tokens per task

### 2. Quality Metrics (The "How Well")
Track AI output quality over time:
- **RAG Retrieval Confidence:** Are retrieved documents relevant?
- **Answer Grounding Rate:** What % of answers cite source documents?
- **Hallucination Rate:** Measured via sampling + human review
- **Task Completion Rate:** Did the agent achieve the user's goal?

### 3. Reasoning Traces (The "Why")
Replay agent decision-making step by step:
- Log every tool call with inputs, outputs, and timestamps
- Record which documents were retrieved and their relevance scores
- Capture the full reasoning chain for auditable decision-making

### 4. Business Impact Metrics (The "So What")
Connect AI performance to outcomes:
- **Deflection Rate:** % of issues resolved without human escalation
- **Customer Satisfaction:** CSAT scores correlated to AI interactions
- **Time Saved:** Hours per week recovered from automated tasks
- **Cost per Resolution:** AI cost vs. human cost for same task
