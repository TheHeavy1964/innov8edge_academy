## Your AIOps Capstone: Building the Playbook

### Why a Playbook?
The difference between an AI hobbyist and an AI professional is documentation. A Playbook signals to your organization, your clients, and regulators that your AI operations are intentional, auditable, and controlled.

### Playbook Structure

#### Section 1: AI Portfolio Inventory
| System Name | Purpose | Model/Vendor | Owner | Phase | Data Sources |
|------------|---------|-------------|-------|-------|-------------|
| [Your AI systems here] | | | | | |

#### Section 2: Lifecycle & Deployment Standards
- **Staging requirements:** [Define your minimum staging criteria]
- **Canary threshold:** [What % of traffic, for how long]
- **Rollback trigger:** [Specific metrics/thresholds]
- **Change review process:** [Who approves production promotions]

#### Section 3: Monitoring Dashboard
- **Performance metrics:** [Latency, error rate, throughput]
- **Quality metrics:** [RAG confidence, task completion rate]
- **Cost metrics:** [Tokens per task, cost per request]
- **Alert thresholds:** [Define what fires an alert]
- **Escalation path:** [Who gets paged and when]

#### Section 4: Eval Framework
- **Golden datasets:** [Where stored, how maintained]
- **Eval types:** [Correctness, quality, regression, A/B]
- **CI/CD gate:** [What score blocks promotion]
- **Review cadence:** [How often full evals are reviewed]

#### Section 5: Security Posture
- **Threat model:** [Your top 3 AI-specific threats]
- **Defense layers:** [Your current stack]
- **Least-privilege map:** [Tool permissions per agent]
- **Security incident runbook:** [Steps 1-5 when a breach is detected]

#### Section 6: Governance & Policy
- **AI Use Policy location:** [Link]
- **Accountability matrix:** [Decision → Owner]
- **Regulatory compliance:** [Applicable regulations + status]
- **Audit schedule:** [Quarterly/biannual review dates]

#### Section 7: Scaling Architecture
- **Current pattern:** [Hub-spoke / Pipeline / Peer]
- **Model routing strategy:** [Which tasks go to which models]
- **Monthly AI spend:** [$X target vs $Y current]
- **Cost optimization backlog:** [Prioritized improvements]
