# Building Your First Agent

Welcome to the **Builder Track**. You are no longer just using AI; you are engineering **Autonomy**.

## From Chat to Agency
A standard AI interaction is **Reactive**: you ask, it answers.
An Agent interaction is **Proactive**: you give a goal, and the agent determines the steps to get there.

### The Agentic Core:
1. **Perception:** Reading the user's goal and the current environment.
2. **Reasoning:** Planning the sequence of actions.
3. **Action:** Executing a tool (API, Script, etc.).
4. **Observation:** Learning from the result of that action.

## Your First Milestone
In the sandbox today, you will define your agent's **System Prompt** (its personality and rules) and its **Toolbelt** (what it is allowed to do). 

> **Remember:** A powerful agent with a bad plan is just a fast way to make a big mistake. Planning is everything.
