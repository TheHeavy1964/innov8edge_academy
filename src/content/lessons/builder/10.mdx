# Multi-Agent Orchestration: The Power of Teams

The most powerful AI systems today aren't single models—they are **Squads**. Orchestration is the art of managing these teams.

## Why Teams Beat Individuals

A single AI trying to "Build a Business" will eventually get overwhelmed by details and lose track of the big picture. A squad of 5 agents divides the labor:
1. **The Visionary:** Keeps track of the high-level roadmap.
2. **The Researcher:** Gathers the necessary data.
3. **The Builder:** Implements the solution.
4. **The Tester:** Looks for bugs and edge cases.
5. **The Publisher:** Handles the final delivery.

### Common Orchestration Patterns:
- **Hierarchical:** Manager -> Workers. Best for complex, quality-critical tasks.
- **Sequential:** Line-up of agents. Best for content pipelines.
- **Joint-Reasoning:** Agents "discuss" a problem in a shared space to find the best solution.

### The "Squad" Advantage:
- **Reliability:** Errors are caught by 'Reviewer' agents before they reach the user.
- **Scalability:** You can add more 'Worker' agents to handle more data.
- **Modularity:** You can swap out a 'Coder' agent for a better model without changing the rest of the team.

> **Master Tip:** Treat your Agent Squad like a real department. Give them clear roles, specific KPIs, and a defined communication channel.
